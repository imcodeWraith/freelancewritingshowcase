Our Mission

This site was designed with the goal of showcasing gifted writers' abilities and connecting them to opportunities with potential clients as well as networking with other writers. All levels of writers are welcomed to join to improve their craft, seek information, and receive helpful feedback. Let's build this community and lift everyone up. We're all going to make it.

Submission Guidelines

We accept:

  • Short stories (up to 5,000 words)
  • Novel excerpts (first chapters or standout scenes)
  • Poetry (up to 3 pieces)
  • Creative nonfiction

Email submissions to imcodewraith@gmail.com with the subject: Writer Showcase Submission. Include a brief bio and any relevant links to your social media or portfolio.
