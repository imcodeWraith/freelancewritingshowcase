Mission

This site was designed with the goal of showcasing gifted writers' abilities and connecting them to opportunities with potential clients as well as networking with other writers. All levels of writers are welcomed to join to improve their craft, seek information, and helpful feedback. Let's build this community and lift everyone up. We're all going to make it.

About the Founder

imcodeWraith

As a lifelong storyteller and tech enthusiast, I blend my expertise in computer coding with a deep passion for creative writing to inspire and connect writers worldwide. Under the pen name imcodeWraith, I’ve spent years honing my craft as a hobbyist writer, driven by my love for reading and the transformative power of stories. To me, stories are time machines—transporting us to new worlds, helping us lose or find ourselves, and showcasing the artistry of the written word.

Frustrated by the lack of platforms that truly serve writers’ needs, I founded Freelance Writers Spotlight to create a vibrant community where creators can sharpen their skills, showcase their talents, and connect directly with job opportunities. With a particular fondness for sci-fi, fantasy, romance, and horror, I’m dedicated to empowering writers across genres to elevate their craft and build meaningful careers. Join me in celebrating the art of storytelling and forging a network that amplifies every writer’s voice.
